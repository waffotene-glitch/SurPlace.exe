The Flow

How SurPlace Works

Three simple steps between you and your next great meal.

01

Snap it live

Take a real-time photo or video of your meal in the restaurant — no old uploads allowed.

02

Rate & review

Give the plate or restaurant a star rating and write what made it special (or not).

03

Help others discover

Your review joins the live feed so others nearby can make smarter dining decisions.

Restaurant reviews Live customer feedback on dining experiences
Plate reviews Showcase dishes with photos and ratings
Trusted discovery Find the best meals around you
